    Quality and sustainability


    We tailor fit scientifically-supported nutritional supplements to each individual's body with clean and sustainable ingredients.

    Rigorous Science

    Mendel's is made up of a team of scientists. We question everything. We make sure everything we use and make for our customers are backed up by peer-reviewed science. We ensure the accuracy by using stringent protocols at our state-of-the-art testing lab at The University of British Columbia. We combine the accuracy of today's molecular genetics with the simplicity of our designs to provide members with the easiest way to get useful insights about the body.

    Molecular Refinement

    We carefully source ingredients that stand up to stringent quality criteria. Mendel's is about knowing exactly what to put into the body, based on what the body actually needs. We don't tolerate the unknowns. We don't assume, we verify the purity and safety of each compound.

    Our sources are high quality, ethical, sustainable, and are approved by the FDA and Health Canada. Your personalized supplements are produced in compliance with the Dietary Supplement Current Good Manufacturing Practice (DS CGMP). The ingredients are all naturally occurring, and we take an extra step to ensure they are purified and chemically defined.

    You test yourself. We test ourselves too. Extensive quality testing extends to every aspect at Mendel's.

    Sustainability

    We want to help our members achieve wellness at the same time as being responsible global citizens. This means being environmentally conscious in our designs, by selecting green ingredients and materials. We set ethical principals for our business relationships to ensure our actions are not infringing on social justice and equal opportunity.

    That's why we've designed all of our packaging to be 100% biodegradable and compostable, to help our members, and the planet, stay healthy.
